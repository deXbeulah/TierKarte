Firefly

Fireflies produce light through an efficient chemical reaction that allows them to glow without wasting heat energy. For fireflies, 100% of the energy goes into making light.

  • Scientific Name: Lampyridae
  • Average length: 3 centimeters
  • Average span: 2 months
  • Habiat: Forest

Like all beetles, fireflies undergo complete metamorphosis with four stages in their life cycle: egg, larva, pupa, and adult.
